What We're Building

Three Systems. One Platform. Zero Complexity.

No platform combines financial management, student information, and AI coaching for small schools and K-12 programs. We're building the first.

💰

Financial Management

Tuition tracking, expense categorization, cash flow forecasting, and budget tools, designed for how small schools actually handle money.

🎓

Student Information System

Enrollment, attendance, family communication, and compliance documentation, all in one place instead of scattered across five different apps.

🤖

AI Coaching (Meet Hank)

Think of Hank as the CFO friend you wish you had. He explains your finances in plain English, flags problems before they become emergencies, and coaches you through every decision.

* Financial Management currently integrates with QuickBooks Online as the accounting platform.
